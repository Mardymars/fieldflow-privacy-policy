FieldFlow Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 26, 2026

FieldFlow ("we", "our", or "the app") is an independently developed application created by Adel Marcano.

FieldFlow is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the Watch Tower Bible and Tract Society of Pennsylvania or any related legal entities.

1. Information We Collect

  • Authentication identifiers through Firebase Authentication: anonymous UID, email-format login identifier and password credential, or phone number (if phone login is used)
  • We do not require email verification
  • Email-style login may use an alias/username formatted like an email address
  • User-generated content such as streets, addresses, notes, congregation information, assignments, reminders, follow-ups, and submissions
  • Device/browser local app data stored in local storage (such as settings, drafts, tags, calendar cache, and congregation context)
  • Optional backup files created by users may include fieldflow:* local data and user hints (such as UID, email, or phone when present)

2. How Information Is Used

  • To authenticate users and keep data associated with the correct account
  • To synchronize app data across devices
  • To provide reminders/notifications and core app functionality
  • To support official congregation workflows, including manager/founder review of submitted content
  • To maintain reliability and resolve technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

FieldFlow uses Google Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore.

FieldFlow also stores some app data in local device/browser storage.

Data sent to Firebase is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections and stored within Firebase infrastructure.

Backup files are stored wherever the user chooses to save them.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, trade, or monetize user data.

Data may be processed by infrastructure providers necessary to operate the app, such as Google Firebase.

Users may intentionally share exported content using device/browser share features.

In official congregation workflows, submitted content may be visible to authorized managers/founders for review.

5. Intended Use

FieldFlow is primarily intended to assist members of Jehovah's Witnesses in organizing field ministry information. The app may also be used by the general public for lawful personal organizational purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

FieldFlow is not directed toward children under age 13 and is not intended for child use.

7. Account and Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data by emailing adeljevan@gmail.com with the subject line "FieldFlow Account Deletion Request".

To help locate records, include your Firebase UID if available.

Upon verified request, data associated with that account will be deleted from active systems within 30 days where reasonably possible.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the application after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
