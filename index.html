FieldFlow Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

FieldFlow ("we", "our", or "the app") is an independently developed mobile application created by Adel Marcano. The app is designed to assist individuals in organizing and managing field ministry records and related personal information.

FieldFlow is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the Watch Tower Bible and Tract Society of Pennsylvania or any related legal entities.

1. Information We Collect

  • A system-generated anonymous user identifier (Firebase UID)
  • User-generated content such as streets, addresses, notes, congregation information, and follow-ups
  • Basic device data necessary for app functionality

FieldFlow does not require verified email addresses, passwords, or personal identity information for use.

2. How Information Is Used

  • To associate stored data with a specific anonymous user account
  • To synchronize user data securely across devices
  • To maintain application functionality
  • To improve performance and resolve technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

FieldFlow uses Google Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication (anonymous sign-in) and Cloud Firestore, to securely store and manage data.

All data is transmitted using encrypted HTTPS connections and stored within Firebase infrastructure.

User data remains under the control of the user and is not accessed for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, trade, or monetize personal data.

User data is not shared with third parties except as necessary to operate the application through secure infrastructure providers such as Google Firebase.
