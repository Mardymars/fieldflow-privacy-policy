FieldFlow Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

FieldFlow ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the FieldFlow mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address (for authentication purposes)
  • User-generated content such as streets, addresses, notes, and follow-ups
  • Basic device and usage data required for app functionality

2. How We Use Information

  • To securely authenticate users
  • To store and synchronize data using Firebase services
  • To improve app performance and reliability
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

FieldFlow uses Google Firebase services for authentication and database storage. Data is transmitted using encrypted HTTPS connections.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Data is shared only with service providers necessary for app functionality, such as Google Firebase.

5. Children's Privacy

FieldFlow is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. User Rights

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The effective date will reflect the latest revision.
